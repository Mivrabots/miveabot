Commands

  • !warn <member> [reason] - Warn a member
  • !warnings <member> - View warnings for a member
  • !clearwarnings <member> - Clear all warnings for a member
  • !lock - Lock the server
  • !unlock - Unlock the server
  • !nick <member> [new_nickname] - Change the nickname of a member
  • !echo [message] - Make the bot say something
  • !rps [choice] - Play rock-paper-scissors with the bot
  • !eightball [question] - Ask the magic 8-ball a question
  • !coinflip - Flip a coin
  • !roll [sides] - Roll a die
  • !cat - Get a random cat picture
  • !dog - Get a random dog picture
  • !meme - Get a random meme
  • !joke - Get a random joke
  • !quote - Get a random quote
  • !tictactoe <opponent> - Play tic-tac-toe with another member
  • !botcommands - Show this menu
  • !modlog - View the moderation log
  • !ban <member> [reason] - Ban a member
  • !unban <member_id> - Unban a member
  • !kick <member> [reason] - Kick a member
  • !mute <member> [reason] - Mute a member
  • !unmute <member> - Unmute a member
